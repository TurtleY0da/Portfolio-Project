    Tech Tree Planner Help


    How to move around the view

    You can move around your view by pressing and holding the right click button (or two fingers on a trackpad) while over the viewport, and then drag to move around


    How to add a new item

    There are two options:

    • A top level item

    • A child item

    A top level item is created via the pink button labelled + in the top right corner of the viewport. A popup should appear, allowing you to specify the title, description, and theoretical cost of your item. Upon pressing "Done", a new item will be created in the viewport, with no parent.

    It is now possible to add a child item, via the grey button labelled + on the right side of the new top level item. Once again, a popup should appear, permitting you to specify a title, description, and cost. Additional children can be added to either top level items, or any child item.


    How to modify pre-existing items

    You can change the order of items by pressing either the downwards or upwards arrows in the top left of an item.
    You can also edit the contents of any item by pressing the pencil button in the top left corner of any item, opening a popup to edit the contents. Additionally, if you wish to delete an item entirely, you can press the garbage can button in the edit popup. Note that this process Cannot Be Reversed.
